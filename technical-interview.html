✨ Technical Interview Preparation ✨

Tips for Technical Interviews

✔ Understand the job role and required skills.
✔ Revise fundamentals of Data Structures & Algorithms.
✔ Practice problem-solving regularly on coding platforms.
✔ Communicate your thought process clearly.
✔ Stay calm and manage time effectively during tests.

How to Prepare

✅ Brush up on core subjects like OOP, DBMS, OS, CN.
✅ Practice coding daily on platforms like LeetCode & Codeforces.
✅ Work on personal projects and showcase them on GitHub.
✅ Prepare for system design basics if applying for senior roles.
✅ Mock interviews help you gain real confidence.
