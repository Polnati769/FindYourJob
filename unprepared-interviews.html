Quick Interview Tips
  • Dress slightly more formal than company culture.
  • Start with a confident 20–30s pitch.
  • Use STAR method for answers.
  • Ask thoughtful questions.
How To Prepare
  1. Research company & role.
  2. Map your experience to their needs.
  3. Prepare STAR stories.
  4. Do mock interviews.
